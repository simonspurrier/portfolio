About

Composer and sound artist dropping in on music in debt to landscape and scenery yet bypassing the comfort of portraying the undeniable, drafting expressions that transcend literal representation instead of dressing up language.

Devoted to a personalized synthetization between schematic rhythms and acoustic timbre, pairing a variety of time signatures to produce a surplus portion of off-beats that evacuates standards, to diffuse the notion of where a musical phrase takes off or ends and extend the stretch of it. A musical clutter that spatially orbit in spirals rather than get carried through shut cycles (as for polyrhythms). If every tone is an echo, you got all the possibilities to expand that vicinity which music’s in.

Based in Stockholm, Sweden. Education at Gotland School of Music Composition, Academy of Music and Drama, Gothenburg and Malmö Academy of Music.
